PRIVACY POLICY

Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our website.

  • What personal information do we collect from the people?

    While conversing with our messenger bot, you may be asked to enter your name, email address, mailing address, educational background, date of birth, salary, profession, quality of life in your area for instance quality of water, quality of air among other details to help you with your experience.

  • When do we collect information?

    We collect information from you only when you respond to the questions by the chatbot.

  • How do we use your information?

    The main purpose of collecting this information is to collate and analyse the collected data to develop a data bank as well as to get a better understanding regarding the conditions of living and quality of life in different areas in India. 

  • How do we protect your information?

    We never ask for credit card numbers or any other financial information.
    Further, all personal information is secured with continuous and requisite security measures and further it is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems and are obligated to keep the information confidential. 

    We implement a variety of security measures when a user enters, submits, or accesses their information, to maintain the safety of your personal information. Further, the name and number is stored only if consent has been provided. All the data is converted and secured through encryption.

  • Do we use 'cookies'?

    We do not use cookies for tracking or any other purpose whatsoever. 

  • Third-party disclosure

    The data collected is not shared/sold/transferred to any third party. The analysed data will be shared, however, no personal identification information (attributing back to the user) whatsoever, is shared/transferred to any third party or anyone whatsoever.

    We do not specifically market to children under the age of 13 years old.
